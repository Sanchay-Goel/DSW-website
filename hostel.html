Hostels

The Diamond Hostel is an eye-soothing centre of attraction of IIT (ISM) Dhanbad. It is the oldest yet one of the best hostels in the campus, housing the final and superfinal year students of various academic programmes. The hostel boasts of typical colonial architecture, rendering it our institute's valuable heritage. It was built in 1926, thus exhibiting an appealing vintage look. It has 15-feet tall rooms with spacious corridors offering a comfortable living to its residents. The hostel also has its own gym facility for the students. It is located at the very centre of the campus, with the lecture hall, canteen and auditorium at just a few paces away.
